Sheppard's Pie Recipe

Ingredients

  • Some veggies
  • Some sauce
  • Some cheese
  • Some spice

Instructions

  1. Mix up the veggies and sauce
  2. Put the the mix into an oven container
  3. Sprinkle the cheese on top and add spices
  4. Bake until golden brown
  5. Let it cool a bit and then eat it
